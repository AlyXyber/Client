Our Story

Born From Passion,
Served With Love

✦

Wrap Sarap started with one simple dream: to bring delicious, nutritious, and affordable wraps to every corner of Tawi-Tawi. What began as a small stall has grown into a beloved brand that families come back to again and again.

Every wrap is made fresh, packed with wholesome ingredients, and wrapped with the same care we've had since day one. Because at Wrap Sarap — it's not just food, it's family.
